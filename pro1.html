Lokesh Kanagaraj

Lokesh Kanagaraj is an Indian film director and screenwriter who primarily works in Tamil cinema. He was born on June 5, 1986, in Tirupur, Tamil Nadu, India. After completing his graduation in Visual Communication, he started working as an assistant director under various renowned directors in the Tamil film industry. Lokesh Kanagaraj made his directorial debut with the Tamil film "Maanagaram" in 2017, which received critical acclaim and was a commercial success. His second film, "Kaithi" (2019), starring Karthi, was also a critical and commercial success, receiving rave reviews for its unique storyline and gripping screenplay. The movie was remade in Telugu and starred Karthikeya in the lead role.

In 2021, Lokesh Kanagaraj directed the Tamil film "Master," which starred Thalapathy Vijay and Vijay Sethupathi in lead roles. The film was a huge commercial success and broke several box office records in Tamil cinema. It was also released in several other languages and received positive reviews for its action sequences, music, and performances. Lokesh Kanagaraj is known for his realistic storytelling, non-linear narrative style, and intense characterizations. He has gained a reputation for making engaging films that keep the audience hooked till the end. He is considered one of the most promising young directors in the Tamil film industry and has a bright future ahead





Kaithi

Kaithi is a 2019 Tamil-language action thriller film directed by Lokesh Kanagaraj and produced by S. R. Prakashbabu and S. R. Prabhu under the banner Dream Warrior Pictures. The film stars Karthi in the lead role, with Narain, Dheena, Arjun Das, and George Maryan in supporting roles. The movie revolves around the life of a convict named Dilli, who is released from prison on parole to attend his daughter's wedding. However, things take a drastic turn when he is forced to help a group of police officers transport a group of dangerous criminals to a high-security prison. Dilli, who is looking forward to meeting his daughter after years of separation, is caught in a deadly game of cat and mouse with the criminals, who are determined to kill him and escape.

The film received widespread critical acclaim for its unique storyline, gripping screenplay, and powerful performances by the lead actors. It was praised for its realistic portrayal of the police department and the criminal underworld, and the intense action sequences were also highly appreciated by the audience.

Vikram

Vikram is a 2022 Indian Tamil-language action thriller film written and directed by Lokesh Kanagaraj. Lokesh Kanagaraj and Rathna Kumar worked on the dialogues in this film together. It is produced by Kamal Haasan who stars in the title role along with Vijay Sethupathi and Fahadh Faasil with Narain and Kalidas Jayaram in supporting roles. A spiritual successor of the 1986 film also named Vikram, it is the second installment in the Lokesh Cinematic Universe following Kaithi (2019).

Master

Master is a 2021 Indian Tamil-language action-thriller film directed by Lokesh Kanagaraj and starring Vijay and Vijay Sethupathi in the lead roles, with Malavika Mohanan, Arjun Das, Andrea Jeremiah, and Shanthanu Bhagyaraj in supporting roles. The film revolves around John Durairaj (Vijay), a charismatic and alcoholic professor who is sent to a juvenile school for his misconduct.

There he meets Bhavani (Vijay Sethupathi), the ruthless leader of the school's criminal network. What follows is a clash between the two as John tries to reform the students while Bhavani tries to control them. The film received positive reviews from both critics and audiences for its performances, direction, and action sequences. It was also a commercial success, grossing over ₹250 crore worldwide.

Maanagaram

Maanagaram is a 2017 Indian Tamil-language thriller film directed by Lokesh Kanagaraj and produced by Potential Studios. The film stars Sri, Sundeep Kishan, Regina Cassandra, and Charle in the lead roles. The story of the film is set in Chennai and revolves around four main characters whose lives intersect due to a series of incidents that occur in the city. The film explores the themes of urbanization, globalization, and the impact of modernization on human relationships.

The film received critical acclaim for its innovative storytelling, strong performances, and engaging screenplay. It was praised for its realistic portrayal of life in a metropolitan city and its ability to keep the audience on the edge of their seats. The film was also a commercial success and was widely regarded as one of the best Tamil films of 2017.
